Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Speaker Cleaner ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application Speaker Cleaner collects, uses, and protects user information.

1. Information We Collect

Speaker Cleaner does not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. The app is designed to work without requiring personal user data.

However, the application may use third-party services such as advertising networks that may collect certain non-personal information automatically.

2. Advertising

Our app may display advertisements using third-party advertising providers such as Google AdMob. These services may collect non-personal data such as:

  • Device information
  • Advertising identifiers
  • App usage statistics
  • Approximate location (non-precise)

This data helps deliver relevant advertisements and improve ad performance.

3. Permissions Used

The application may request the following permissions:

  • Vibration Permission – Used to run vibration cleaning features.
  • Audio Access – Used to generate sound frequencies to clean speakers.

These permissions are used only for app functionality and are not used to collect personal data.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user privacy. Since our application does not store personal data, there is minimal risk related to personal information.

5. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services that have their own privacy policies, including:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Services

We recommend reviewing their privacy policies for more details.

6. Children's Privacy

Speaker Cleaner is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
