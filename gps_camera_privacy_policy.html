GPS Camera App – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 17, 2026

At GPS Camera App, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how our application collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Camera Access

The app requires camera permission to capture photos with GPS and watermark information.

Location Access

The app requires location permission to add GPS information such as:

  • Address
  • Coordinates
  • Altitude
  • Accuracy
  • Map location

Location data is only used while using the app features and is not shared with third parties.

Storage Access

The app may access device storage to:

  • Save captured photos
  • Display saved images in gallery
  • Allow users to share photos

2. How We Use Information

The collected information is used only for:

  • Capturing GPS-enabled photos
  • Displaying location details on images
  • Saving and organizing photos
  • Providing map-based photo visualization
  • Improving app functionality and user experience

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data.

3. Third-Party Services

The app may use trusted third-party services including:

  • Google Maps SDK
  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Analytics (if enabled)
  • AdMob (if advertisements are enabled)

These services may collect limited technical information according to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user information and stored data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Permissions Used

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Camera Permission
  • Location Permission
  • Storage / Media Permission
  • Internet Permission

These permissions are required only for core app functionality.

7. User Control

Users can:

  • Disable location permission anytime through device settings
  • Delete saved photos from the app
  • Control watermark settings inside the app

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
