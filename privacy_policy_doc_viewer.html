The App is designed to work with documents stored on your device. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the basic features of the App.

When you use the App, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.

1.3 In-App Purchase Information

If you purchase a premium subscription, transaction information is processed by Google Play's billing system. We do not store your payment details. We only receive confirmation of your subscription status from Google Play.

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:
